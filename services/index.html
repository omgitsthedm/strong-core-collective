Questions? We have answers.

Get in touch with Kara

Do I need any Pilates experience before starting?

Not at all! Pilates is for every body, and every session is tailored to your level. Even if you are a complete beginner to working out or coming back from an extended break, SCC is right for you.

What should I bring to my session?

Wear comfortable, fitted clothes you can move in. Grip socks are recommended for reformer sessions. You may bring a towel, and water is always a good idea, though we do have water available in-studio for you!

How long is each session?

Private and semi-private reformer and mat Pilates sessions are typically 50 minutes. Event classes run about 45 minutes, but we are happy to customize this to your needs!

What’s the difference between reformer Pilates and mat Pilates?

Reformer Pilates uses a spring-based machine for added resistance and support, while mat Pilates focuses on bodyweight exercises. Both improve strength, flexibility, and stability.

Can Pilates help if I’m recovering from injury or surgery?

Yes. Kara has specialized training in working with post-injury, post-surgery, prenatal, and postpartum clients. Each session will be adapted to your needs.

Do you offer Pilates during pregnancy?

Absolutely. Pilates can be a safe, supportive way to stay strong during pregnancy. Kara will adapt your sessions to each trimester. In the first trimester, we do want to make sure it is disclosed to and approved by your doctor.

How many times per week should I do Pilates to see results?

Most clients benefit from 2–3 sessions per week, but even one session a week can bring noticeable improvements over time.

Do you offer packages or memberships?

Yes! Packages of 3, 5, or 10 sessions are available at a discounted rate for private Pilates, as well as flexible options for virtual and event sessions.

Can I book a friend or partner to join me?

Definitely, semi-private sessions are available at the Glendale studio, and you can also book small group or event classes in your home or at a location such as a park or Airbnb.

Is my first session really free?

Yes! Your first session includes a consultation and an introductory workout session, allowing you to experience Pilates and determine if SCC is the right fit for you.
